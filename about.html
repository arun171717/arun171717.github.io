About Arun Pillarisetty

Introducing Arun, a cybersecurity enthusiast and senior at the University of Oklahoma. Armed with a solid foundation in Management Information Systems from the Price College of Business, Arun is not just a dedicated student but also a skilled professional in the realm of cybersecurity. Arun specializes in penetration testing, vulnerability management, threat detection, and incident response, showcasing a comprehensive skill set crucial for ensuring the security of digital systems. Beyond cybersecurity, Arun is well-versed in AWS, demonstrating a versatile understanding of cloud computing. Fueled by a passion for cybersecurity, Arun holds certifications such as CompTIA PenTest+, CySA+, Security+, and AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner. These certifications underline Arun's commitment to staying abreast of industry best practices and cutting-edge technologies. As a business professional and aspiring entrepreneur, Arun brings a distinctive perspective to the cybersecurity landscape, seamlessly blending technical expertise with a keen understanding of business management. With a focus on bridging the gap between technology and business objectives, Arun is poised to make a significant impact in the dynamic field of cybersecurity.
